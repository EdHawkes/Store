About Us

We are the online counterpart to the Kennesaw State University Bookstore. Because our campus bookstore is so popular, we decided to create this website so you can spend less time waiting in line for your books and more time acing your homework.


The KSU Online Bookstore is dedicated to providing the same book quality and customer service you will find at the campus bookstore, with the added convenience of ordering wherever you can find a computer.
