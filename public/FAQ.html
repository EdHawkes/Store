FAQ

How is shipping cost calculated?
All orders that include a physical copy of a book will have a flat fee of $14.99 for shipping.

Where do you deliver to?
Anywhere in the United States that USPS ships to.

Do you accept returns?
All returns for the physical book purchases can be taken to the on-campus bookstore at KSU, the online site itself does handle returns.

What payment methods do you accept?
Financial Aid, Paypal, Visa/Mastercard, no split payments are allowed.

Do I have to be a KSU student to purchase books?
No, anyone can purchase book.

Will I get charged tax for my purchase?
Yes, we use the 7% Georgia Tax fee on all orders.

If I purchase an eBook, how can I access it?
After an eBook is purchased, you will be instantly given a receipt VIA email and on the confirmation page that include the link to your eBook.

Can I order book anytime throughout the year?
No, we only handle orders at the beginning of phase 1 registration until the 15th class day of when each semester starts.
